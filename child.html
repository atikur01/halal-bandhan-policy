Child Safety & CSAE Policy

HalalBandhan is committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), including Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).

Our Commitment

  • Any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation is strictly prohibited.
  • Users are not allowed to create, upload, share, or distribute CSAM in any form.
  • Accounts found violating child safety policies are permanently banned.

Reporting & Moderation

  • Users can report child safety concerns directly from within the HalalBandhan app.
  • All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.
  • Violating content is removed immediately upon verification.

Legal Compliance

HalalBandhan complies with all applicable local, regional, and international child protection laws. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and report confirmed CSAM cases to relevant authorities as required by law.
