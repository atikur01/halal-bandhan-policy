1. Introduction

Welcome to Halal Bandhan ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Islamic matrimony mobile application.

Our Commitment: Halal Bandhan is designed with Islamic values at its core. We respect your privacy and ensure that your personal information, especially guardian/mahram details, is protected and only shared with matched users as per Islamic guidelines.

By using Halal Bandhan, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when creating your profile:

  • Account Information: Email address, password
  • Personal Details: Full name, year of birth, gender, skin tone, marital status, height, weight, blood group
  • Location Information: Current address, permanent address
  • Religious Information: Religion, sect
  • Biography: Personal description

2.2 Educational & Professional Information

  • Education level and institution
  • Profession and professional details
  • Monthly income range

2.3 Family Information

  • Parents' status and occupations
  • Number of siblings
  • Financial status

2.4 Guardian/Mahram Information

  • Guardian's name
  • Guardian's phone number

Important: Guardian/Mahram contact information is only visible to users who have mutually matched with you. This ensures the halal nature of communication through proper channels.

2.5 Partner Preferences

  • Expected age range, height, weight preferences
  • Preferred skin tone, education level, marital status
  • Preferred location
  • Additional expectations

2.6 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent
  • FCM Tokens: For push notification delivery
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data for app improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Core Services

  • Create and manage your matrimony profile
  • Display your profile to potential matches of the opposite gender
  • Enable interest sending and receiving
  • Facilitate matching between compatible users
  • Enable in-app messaging between matched users

3.2 Communication

  • Send push notifications for new interests and matches
  • Send important service updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3.4 Safety & Security

  • Prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities
  • Enforce our terms of service
  • Protect the safety of our users

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (excluding guardian details) is visible to registered users of the opposite gender. Guardian/Mahram contact information is only shared with mutually matched users.

4.2 Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting, cloud messaging

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

We Never: Sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Firebase Security Rules: Strict access controls on database operations
  • Authentication: Secure email/password authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Screenshot Prevention: In-app screenshot and screen recording restrictions
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates

Note: While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as follows:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deactivated Accounts: Profile is hidden but data is retained for potential reactivation
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Chat Messages: Retained until the match is deleted or accounts are removed

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access & Portability

You can view all your profile information within the app and request a copy of your data.

7.2 Correction

You can update or correct your profile information at any time through the Edit Profile feature.

7.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us or using the in-app account deletion feature.

7.4 Deactivation

You can temporarily deactivate your account, which hides your profile from other users while preserving your data.

7.5 Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing by deleting your account, though this will result in loss of access to our services.

8. Children's Privacy

Halal Bandhan is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

If we discover that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
